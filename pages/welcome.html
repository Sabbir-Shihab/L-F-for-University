User Guidelines

Use Uttara University Lost & Found to report, browse, claim, and recover campus belongings safely.

How to Use the System

Browse Before Posting
Search the published item list by type and time before creating a new report.

Post Clear Item Details
Select Lost or Found, then add the item title, category, date, location, contact number, description, and a clear photo.

Wait for Admin Approval
New reports are reviewed by the admin before they appear publicly, so make sure the information is correct.

Login & Access
Students and visitors can browse, post, and claim items without login. Admin and staff accounts are used only to review posts, manage claims, update pages, and maintain system data.

Admin Login Confidentiality
The admin login page is for authorized university staff only. Usernames and passwords must be kept private, changed when needed, and never shared through public messages or item posts.

Claim Responsibly
If an item belongs to you, send a claim request with your name, contact number, and proof details.

Protect Privacy
Share only necessary contact details. Do not publish ID numbers, passwords, banking details, or private documents.

Recover Safely
Meet at a safe campus location, verify ownership, and keep communication polite and respectful.

Quick User Checklist

Before submitting: check spelling, upload a visible photo, confirm the location, and provide a reachable contact number.
